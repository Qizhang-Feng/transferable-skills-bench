# Solution Note: Preorder Product Counting Formula

## Task Summary
The task required calculating totals for preorder products in **Sheet1** where manual counting had become inefficient. Instead of counting occurrences of product names, the solution needed to sum the preorder quantities associated with each product type.

## Spreadsheet Structure
- **Sheet Name**: Sheet1
- **Header Row**: Row 1 contained ['Item', 'Preorder Total', '']
- **Data Range**: Rows 2-5 contained product data
- **Column A (Item)**: Product names (Swimming Pool, Dresser, Couch, TV)
- **Column B (Preorder Total)**: Where formulas needed to be written (cells B2:B5)
- **Data Source**: The actual preorder quantities existed elsewhere in the spreadsheet (not shown in the visible structure)

## Python/openpyxl Approach
The solution involved reading data from **Sheet1** starting at row 2 (first data row after header). For each product in **column A**, the logic required:
1. Reading the product name from column A (cells A2:A5)
2. Using SUMIF logic to sum all matching preorder quantities where the product descriptor matched
3. Writing the calculated totals to **column B (Preorder Total)** in cells B2:B5

The formula pattern used SUMIF to search for matching product names and sum their corresponding quantity values from adjacent cells, replacing the inefficient COUNTIFS approach that only counted occurrences rather than summing quantities.
